# Warhammer 40k: Lore of Cadia

## Overview
**Cadia** was the Imperium of Man's most crucial **Fortress World**. Located at the edge of the Eye of Terror, it guarded the **Cadian Gate**—the only navigable, stable route in and out of the Warp-infested region large enough for massive fleet movements. 

## Strategic Importance
*   **The Cadian Gate:** It acted as the primary bulwark holding back the tides of Chaos. Without Cadia, the forces of the Ruinous Powers could spill directly into the heart of the Imperium.
*   **Cadian Pylons:** The planet was seeded with vast, mysterious, black stone monoliths known as the Cadian Pylons. These structures, originally built by the Necrons, generated anti-psychic energy that dampened the Warp and actively pushed back the Eye of Terror. 
*   **Militarized Culture:** Because of its proximity to Chaos, Cadia was permanently on a war footing. Its citizens were trained to fight and resist corruption from the moment they could walk, developing the famous **Cadian Shock Troops**.

## The Cadian Regiments
*   Due to their rigorous discipline and iconic standard-issue gear (flak armor, the Mk.IX helmet, and lasguns), the Cadian regiments became the gold standard of the Astra Militarum.
*   Cadian training was so highly respected that their doctrines and combat tactics were emulated by Imperial Guard units across the entire galaxy.

## History and Key Conflicts
*   **The Horus Heresy:** 40 years before the Heresy, Cadia was a primitive world with a human population that worshipped the Chaos Gods. When Lorgar (Primarch of the Word Bearers) discovered this, he performed a dark ritual and initiated a cyclonic torpedo bombardment, temporarily wiping out the population.
*   **The Black Crusades:** Cadia was the primary target for Abaddon the Despoiler. He launched 13 Black Crusades over ten millennia to weaken the Imperium's defenses and claim the Blackstone Fortresses.

## The Fall of Cadia
*   During the **13th Black Crusade**, Abaddon the Despoiler made his ultimate push to conquer the fortress world. 
*   The Imperial defenders, led by **Lord Castellan Ursarkar E. Creed**, held the line valiantly despite immense casualties.
*   The Chaos forces destroyed the Cadian void shields, allowing Abaddon to crash the crippled Blackstone Fortress (the *Will of Eternity*) directly into the planet's surface.
*   This catastrophic impact shattered Cadia's tectonic plates, destroyed the Pylons, and tore the galaxy apart, creating the **Cicatrix Maledictum** (The Great Rift).

## "Cadia Stands!"
*   Though their homeworld was completely obliterated, the Cadian soldiers did not surrender. 
*   Because countless millions of Cadian Shock Troops were already deployed across the stars fighting in various campaigns, the Cadian culture and military forces survived.
*   They formed new regiments and continued to fight across the galaxy under the defiant rallying cry: **"Cadia Stands!"**
